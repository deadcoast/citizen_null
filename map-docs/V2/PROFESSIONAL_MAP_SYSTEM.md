## **HOW TO USE THE PROFESSIONAL SYSTEM**

### **3D Navigation**
1. **Mouse Controls**:
   - **Left Click + Drag** - Rotate camera around system
   - **Right Click + Drag** - Pan camera
   - **Scroll Wheel** - Zoom in/out

   - **Click Stations** - View station details
